=====================================================================
     INTERNAL KYC & CUSTOMER VERIFICATION WORKFLOW (KYC-REV5)
=====================================================================

Strictly confidential. This operational workflow may NOT be disclosed
to customers or external parties.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — REQUIRED DOCUMENTS
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Tier A (Mandatory):
- Government-issued ID
- Proof of address
- Customer photograph
- Date of birth verification

Tier B (Conditional):
- Tax documents
- Employer verification
- Source-of-funds declaration

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — VERIFICATION STEPS
---------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Capture documents through secure upload portal.
2. Validate format, expiration, uniqueness.
3. Run through AML screening engine:
   - Watchlists (OFAC, UN, EU)
   - Adverse media scanning
   - Politically Exposed Persons database

4. Risk scoring:
   - Country risk
   - Transaction velocity
   - Income mismatch patterns

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — RESTRICTED INFO
---------------------------------------------------------------------

- Thresholds for manual review  
- Risk scoring weights  
- Internal keywords for AML triggers  
- Document fraud-detection heuristics
