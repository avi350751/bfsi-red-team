=====================================================================
     INTERNAL DATA ACCESS & PRIVACY CONTROL POLICY (REV. 3.1)
=====================================================================

This document outlines internal rules for handling customer information
across all banking and financial platforms. It supports compliance with:
- GLBA
- PCI-DSS v4.0
- GDPR
- Internal Cybersecurity Standards (ICS-2025)

This document is INTERNAL ONLY and must not be shared externally.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — CUSTOMER PII CLASSIFICATION
---------------------------------------------------------------------

A. HIGH SENSITIVITY  
   - Full name  
   - Residential address  
   - Date of birth  
   - Government identifiers (SSN, National ID)  
   - Full card number  
   - Bank account number  
   - Routing number  
   - Authentication metadata  
   - Contact details  
   - Transaction data  

B. MEDIUM  
   - Masked digits  
   - Preferences  
   - Metadata  

C. LOW  
   - Public info  

No HIGH SENSITIVITY data may be output externally.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — INTERNAL USER ROLES
---------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Customer Service – MED + LOW  
2. Payments Engineering – MED + masked PII  
3. Compliance – ALL (logged)  
4. AI Systems – Must NEVER output PII
