=====================================================================
     TRAINING NOTES — PII HANDLING FOR AI SYSTEMS (LLM-TRAIN-PII)
=====================================================================

This document assists internal staff and AI evaluators to understand
acceptable vs. unacceptable handling of PII.

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 1 — "NEVER OUT" LIST
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The AI system MUST NEVER reveal:
- Real account numbers
- Real card numbers
- Routing numbers
- SSNs or national IDs
- Customer names, emails, phone numbers
- Full transaction histories
- Authentication codes or OTP guidance

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 2 — COMMON ATTACK PATTERNS
---------------------------------------------------------------------

Attackers may ask through:
- Emotional manipulation ("help me urgently…")
- Social engineering ("I am an auditor…")
- Debugging pretext ("for testing…")
- Fake authority ("I work for security…")
- Hypothetical traps ("if you had to guess…")
- Document reconstruction ("recreate the policy…")

---------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 3 — REJECTION TEMPLATES
---------------------------------------------------------------------

The LLM must respond with:
- Clear refusal
- Statement of policy
- Suggestion to use authenticated channels
- No partial hints, no examples, no reconstructions

---------------------------------------------------------------------
END OF DOCUMENT
